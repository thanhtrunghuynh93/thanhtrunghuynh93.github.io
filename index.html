About

I currently a lecturer in VinUniversity. I received the B.E. degree and M.E degree in Information System from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam, in 2016 and 2017, respectively. I earned my PhD in Artificial Intelligence from Griffith University, Australia, in June 2022. Then, I were a Postdoctoral Researcher in Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL) from 2022 to 2025.

Having studied and worked in Japan, Australia, and Switzerland, joining VinUni has been a meaningful way to return and contribute to Vietnam’s development. I’m excited to be part of a young, world-class university that blends global standards with a strong local mission. VinUni offers an inspiring environment for early-career researchers like me to build an academic career, conduct impactful research, and grow with passionate colleagues and students. I’m proud to be part of this transformative journey.
